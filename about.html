That's me

In 2021, I started the SheCodes Workshops that introduced me to the world of coding. Once I got a glimpse of the power and beauty of that field, I knew that I didn't want to stop. During my journey with SheCodes, I got to code real-life projects, which you can check out on this website. Now that I finished all of the available workshops, I have a solid foundation in the most important front-end technologies that are used today in web development. I am constantly building new things and learning more techniques. As my parents told me: What you can't do, you have to practice! I am dedicated to clean, maintainable and understandable code; you can find many of my projects open-sourced on Github.
